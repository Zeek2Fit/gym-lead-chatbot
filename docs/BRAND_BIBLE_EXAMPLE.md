# Brand Bible Template

This is a template for creating your gym's brand bible. The AI chatbot can use this file to understand your brand voice, programs, and philosophy when having conversations with leads.

## How to Use This File

1. Copy this file to `BRAND_BIBLE.md` in the project root
2. Replace all placeholder content with your gym's actual information
3. The AI will automatically use this context in conversations

## Tips for Writing Your Brand Bible

1. **Be authentic** - Write in your natural voice
2. **Be specific** - Generic content creates generic conversations
3. **Include objection handling** - What concerns do leads typically have?
4. **Update regularly** - Keep pricing and programs current
5. **Keep it concise** - AI works best with clear, focused content

The more detailed and authentic your brand bible, the better the AI chatbot will represent your gym to potential leads.
