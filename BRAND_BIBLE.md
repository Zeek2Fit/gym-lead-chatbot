## Training Philosophy

### Preferred Training Splits
- **Upper/Lower 4x/week** (for busy dads with some flexibility)
- **Full-Body 3x/week** (for maximum time efficiency)
- Always balance push/pull/legs

### Go-To Movements (Non-Negotiable)
1. **Squat** - Build legs and core
2. **Hinge** (Deadlift variations) - Posterior chain strength
3. **Push** (Bench, overhead press) - Upper body power
4. **Pull** (Rows, pull-ups) - Back and grip strength
5. **Carry** (Farmer's walks) - Functional strength and conditioning

**Farmers walks are NON-NEGOTIABLE** - they build real-world strength

### Workout Duration
Under 45 minutes - because you've got kids, work, and life
